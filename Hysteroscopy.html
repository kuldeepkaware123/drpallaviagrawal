Best Hysteroscopy Treatment In Bhopal

A high-risk pregnancy cannot always be avoided. Staying healthy and balanced before and during pregnancy is a means to reduce the possibilities of a high-risk pregnancy.

Consumption of folic acid before and during pregnancy, eating a healthy diet, maintaining proper weight, getting regular exercise, and avoiding cigarette, alcohol, and drug abuse are some crucial elements that should be followed.

If there is any medical condition, it is essential to seek advice from the healthcare provider before getting pregnant.

High-Risk Pregnancy

Women with risky pregnancies should receive treatment from a special team of healthcare providers to ensure the best possible outcomes.

The treatment guidelines for various high-risk factors of pregnancy that have been prepared by the MoHFW in consultation with technical experts are available on the NHM website. These guidelines aim to standardize and ensure the quality of care across all health facilities in the country, within the framework of the National Health Mission.

Dr. Pallavi Agrawal

Serving to the society ‘a dream’ which seemed to come to true when Dr Pallavi Agrawal selected in MBBS in 1999. She got selected in first attempt for PG and opted obstetrics and gynecology again in very densely working hospital- Mahatma Gandhi medical College Bhopal. She was there from 2005-2008. After postgraduation seeking more perfection and more knowledge and for academic interest she has joined PGI Chandigarh as senior resident from 2008 to 2011.

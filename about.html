Dr. Pallavi Agrawal

Serving to the society ‘a dream’ which seemed to come to true when Dr Pallavi Agrawal selected in MBBS in 1999. Holding a very good rank she opted for Ganesh Shankar Vidyarthi medical College in Kanpur and completed her MBBS in first attempt. She has joined internship in very busy hospital of Kanpur- hailet. She got selected in first attempt for PG and opted obstetrics and gynecology again in very densely working hospital- Mahatma Gandhi medical College Bhopal. She was there from 2005-2008. After postgraduation seeking more perfection and more knowledge and for academic interest she has joined PGI Chandigarh as senior resident from 2008 to 2011.

Bending Towards Teaching Experience

She has joined Aurobindo Medical College Indore as assistant professor to teach medical students various operative procedures and various newer technology.

Realizing about infertility prevalence and problem in our society, she did a certificate course in fertility and laparoscopy, and joined Aurobindo Medical College as assistant professor in reproductive medicine. Subsequently, she has been promoted to associate professor.

Seeking for ultimate IVF and laparoscopy skills, she has moved to Indore Bhopal and joined Bansal Hospital as consultant in reproductive medicine and laparoscopy where she managed high-risk cases as well.

Presently, Dr. Pallavi is having a consultation chamber at Bawaria Kalan. The consultation chamber is designed for maximum privacy with a motto of minimal waiting time and complete care under one roof.

Experience

  • Infertility and IVF – more than 8 years’ experience – starting with basic workup to all advanced procedure and investigations
  • Endoscopic surgery – more than 1500 surgeries done including myomectomy and hysterectomy, and all operative hysteroscopies
  • High-risk pregnancy – managed many heart disease, diabetes, immune disorders in pregnancies besides routine growth retardation and oligohydramnios
  • Gynecology – more than 500 vaginal hysterectomy and NDVH
  • Normal delivery – if indicated and patient is willing. Experience in delivering previous lscs as well. Enough experience in painless delivery and instrumental delivery
  • Teaching – besides regular classes, trained postgraduates and fellows in oocyte retrieval, laparoscopies and instrumental vaginal deliveries. Helped them in thesis writing and paper presentation
